Overview

Purpose

As a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saint I can see the necessity to have informed the members of the ward about what activities have each organization and how the members have to be involved. Some families have kids in primary, young men or young girls and it is important to have the information in the computers, tablets, or phones. And at the same time we need to give the sacrament meeting program electronically. This project is also to full fill what we read in D. and C. 69:3, “And also That he shall continue in writing and making a histoy of all the important things which he shall observe and know concerning my church;” and D&C 69:5 “And also, my servants who are abroad in the earth should send forth the accounts of their stewardships to the land of Zion;”

Audience

Take the information to each member of the organization who has a computer, tablet, or smart phone.
